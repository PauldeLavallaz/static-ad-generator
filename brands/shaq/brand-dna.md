# BRAND DNA — SHAQ Argentina

BRAND DNA DOCUMENT
==================

## BRAND OVERVIEW

- **Name:** SHAQ (Shaquille O'Neal Footwear)
- **Tagline:** "ROMPÉ EL TABLERO. ROMPÉ EL JUEGO." / "Pisá Fuerte"
- **Sub-tagline:** "Advanced Basquet Shoes" / "La Leyenda"
- **Argentine Launch Slogan:** "Shaq Shoes llegó a Argentina. UNA NUEVA POTENCIA PARA EL BASKET NACIONAL."
- **Design Heritage:** Shaquille O'Neal's original brand from the 90s — now reborn as accessible performance streetwear
- **Voice Adjectives:** Dominante, Auténtico, Accesible, Callejero, Nostálgico
- **Positioning:** Performance basketball sneaker with 90s NBA legacy — affordable for the Argentine street market
- **Target:** Males 16–25, Argentine barrio/trap culture, TikTok & Instagram-native, basketball fans and urban streetwear adopters
- **Competitive Differentiation:** Unlike Nike/Adidas (aspirational-luxury, expensive), SHAQ is the king that comes to you. Legacy and quality without pricing you out. "El rey accesible."

---

## VISUAL SYSTEM

- **Primary Color:** Deep royal blue / navy (#0A1E5E) — SHAQ's classic NBA color
- **Secondary Color:** Vibrant gold / yellow (#F5A623) — Shaq's championship gold
- **Accent Color:** Bright white (#FFFFFF) — clean courts, clean kicks
- **Background Colors:** Navy blue, street-concrete gray, off-white cream for premium feel
- **CTA Color:** Gold (#F5A623) with navy text — high contrast Argentine market
- **Typography Style:** Bold condensed sans-serif headlines (reminiscent of NBA trading card fonts) — wide-tracked uppercase for attitude
- **Logo treatment:** "SHAQ" in blocky athletic lettering, often with star/ball detail

---

## PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION

- **Lighting:** High contrast. Street-level practical lighting (harsh Buenos Aires midday sun, nighttime streetlamps, or single dramatic spotlight). NOT soft studio light.
- **Color Grading:** Warm-leaning for street scenes. Cold/high-contrast for action/hero shots. 90s-tinged saturation for nostalgia plays.
- **Composition:** Low-angle power shots, extreme close-ups of sneaker detail, unexpected juxtapositions (sneakers in unlikely Buenos Aires settings)
- **Subject Matter:** Argentine youth (16–25, barrio/trap aesthetic), Buenos Aires urban environments (murales, paredones, potrero, callejón, vereda de Villa Crespo/Flores/Palermo)
- **Props and Surfaces:** Cracked cement, basketball, graffiti walls, chain-link fence, asphalt with puddles at night, vintage NBA posters, Argentine murales
- **Mood:** Dominance without arrogance. Street pride. The king is here and he comes from your barrio.

## PRODUCT DETAILS

### HOF (Hall of Fame) — White/Gold
- White and gold colorway — clean, premium, prestigious
- High-top silhouette, basketball heritage
- Gold SHAQ logo accent
- Best for: premium/editorial shots, god-tier angle, Hall of Fame visual storytelling

### 4X — White/Gold
- Bold white with gold contrast sole
- Athletic performance design
- SHAQ's iconic #32-inspired design DNA
- Best for: clean hero shots, lifestyle, street-casual

### Radiate — Black/Colorful
- Dark base with radiating color accents
- Street-forward design, most "trap" of the line
- Best for: night scenes, barrio energy, youth-led content

### Shaqnotize — Mixed
- Multi-color "hypnotizing" pattern
- Playful, bold, unexpected
- Best for: fun/irreverent content, youth-skewed

## AD CREATIVE STYLE

- **Typical formats:** Action/lifestyle-first, product as the hero, bold typographic callouts
- **Text overlay style:** Bold condensed uppercase on dark backgrounds, gold accents, NO small print as hero
- **Photo vs illustration:** Primarily photo — but retro/illustrated elements (trading card frames, NBA 90s graphics) work extremely well as overlays
- **UGC usage:** High resonance with Argentine trap youth — "pibe de barrio" content feels more authentic than polished studio
- **Offer presentation:** "3 cuotas sin interés con Mercado Pago" + free shipping — weave this into conversion ads naturally
